Chicken Alfredo

Description

My favorite Chicken Alfredo Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccini pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of water. Add fettuccini and cook for 10 minutes or al dente. Drain
  2. Heat butter in a skillet, add chicken diced chicken breast and cook until browned. Season w/ garlic and pepper. Add cream and stir until the noodles thicken. Add parmesan cheese.
