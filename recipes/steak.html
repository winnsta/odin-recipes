Perfectly Reverse Seared Steak

Description

A simple recipe for searing a perfectly cooked steak to your desired liking!

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) rib-eye or ny strip steaks
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 225 degrees F
  2. Place steaks on a baking sheet lined w/ aluminum foil. Season both steaks (on both sides) w/ salt and pepper.
  3. Place the steaks in the oven after preheating. Bake for 40 minutes, periodically checking the internal temperature until it reaches 120 degrees F (for medium-rare, else adjust accordingly).
  4. Add oil to a skillet, and set to medium-high on stovetop. Once the oil starts smoking, add steaks to skillet. Cook 2 minutes on each side.
  5. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
